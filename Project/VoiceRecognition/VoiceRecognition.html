Project#5 Voice Recognition

Hi. I am Alexa Junior. I will change how I look after hearing some keywords. Click Start to begin. Click Stop to stop and Click cancel to cancel voice recognition.
After pressed start button, you can say COLOR, followed by a color, to set the color of the circle. You can say BACKGROUND, followed by a color, to set the color of the background.
You can also say SIZE, followed by a number from 1 to 300, to set the diameter of the circle. If you say the word, HELP, you can hear the above magic spell from me.
In addition, you can say the word, ABOUT, to hear me introduce myself.
